Documentary film project "War has changed" :

We united for the sake of illustrating the life of creative people, as we have lost from Khmelnytsky and continue to help the ZSU, volunteering and boosting the fighting spirit of our creativity. It is important for us to show that to convey to our defenders that the creative potential of Ukraine is left to harrow the country on its own front. Our project is about the history of talented actors, directors, artists, DJs and musicians, who continue to create in Ukraine during the hours of war.

ABOUT US

We are an organization that helps refugees from eastern Ukraine to live. We raise funds for medicines, food, clothing, baby food, provide shelter for overnight stays, help refugees leave the country. We also provide resources for territorial defense and the army.

Donations to us will help to:

  1. Approach our victory
  2. Find a shelter for the refugees from the east of the country
  3. Support women and children that are captured in the bomb shelters.
  4. Support other ONGs
  5. Support Ukrainian Army
